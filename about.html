About Us

Welcome to our Workout Tracker project! This website was created as part of our CPIS-358 course to help users easily organize and keep track of their daily workouts.

Our goal is to provide a simple and clean interface that allows users to:

  • View common exercises
  • Add their own workout details
  • Track reps, sets, and difficulty levels
  • Review their daily activity

We believe that keeping track of your exercise routine helps you stay motivated, consistent, and focused on your fitness goals.

Thank you for using our Workout Tracker!
